Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28.02.2025

Introduction

Thank you for using Combine URL. Your privacy is important to us. This document outlines what data our extension collects and how it is used.

Permissions and Data Usage

Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage permission to save your base URL and prefix. This is required to provide functionality that allows you to quickly navigate to constructed URLs.

Context Menus

The extension uses the contextMenus permission to allow users to right-click on selected text and navigate to the constructed URL. This functionality does not collect or transmit any data.

Data Collection and Sharing

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data related to your configuration is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Security

Since the extension does not collect or transmit data, there are no security concerns related to data exposure. Your configured settings remain private and local to your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
